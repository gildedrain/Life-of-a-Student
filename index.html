    Intro

    Life as a student at Onondaga Community College is a journey filled with experiences both in and out of the classroom. There's so much to think about and so many opportunities -- whether it's academics, financial aid, athletics, volunteering, extracurriculars, or more. You can explore your interests, discover your inherent talents, and transform your possibilities.

    This website can help you navigate through the intricacies of college life and keep you on the path to success at Onondaga and beyond. Use this resource as a guide for your Onondaga journey and all it has to offer.
